Dear Sanmay,

It is my distinct pleasure to inform you, on behalf of the Admissions Committee of the University of California, Irvine, that you have been admitted to UC Irvine for the Fall 2026 academic year. You have been offered admission to the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences as a candidate for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

This year, UC Irvine received over 85,000 undergraduate applications — among the most competitive in the institution's history. Our Admissions Committee conducts a thorough, holistic review of every file, evaluating academic preparation, intellectual range, personal achievement, and demonstrated potential to contribute to our campus community. You distinguished yourself among this exceptionally talented group of applicants, and we are confident that you will thrive here and leave a lasting impact on the Anteater community.

Conditions of Admission
  1. Satisfactory completion of all courses currently in progress, with no significant decline in academic performance from the record upon which this offer was based.
  2. Graduation from your current academic institution with all degree and diploma requirements fulfilled.
  3. Submission of official final transcripts, sent directly from your institution, no later than July 1, 2026.

To formally accept this offer and confirm your intention to enroll, you must submit your Statement of Intent to Register (SIR) no later than May 1, 2026, along with the required enrollment confirmation deposit of $200.00. This deadline is firm; failure to respond by May 1, 2026 will result in the forfeiture of your offer of admission. Your SIR may be submitted through your MyUCI Applicant Portal.

Information regarding your financial aid award, housing application, and mandatory orientation registration will be made available through your portal in the coming weeks. We encourage you to monitor your portal and respond to all outstanding action items promptly to secure the best possible placement and support for your first year.

On behalf of every member of the UCI community — faculty, staff, and your future fellow Anteaters — congratulations on this achievement. You have worked hard to reach this moment, and we are proud to extend this offer. We look forward to welcoming you to Irvine this fall.
