Dear Sanmay,

On behalf of the Chancellor and the faculty of the University of California, San Diego, I am honored to inform you that the Office of Admissions is pleased to offer you admission to UC San Diego for the Fall 2026 academic year. You have been admitted to the Jacobs School of Engineering as a candidate for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering.

UC San Diego is consistently ranked among the world's premier public research universities, and admission to our undergraduate program is among the most selective in the University of California system. This year, we received applications from more than 90,000 prospective students. Following a rigorous, holistic review of each file — weighing academic achievement, intellectual promise, personal character, and potential for contribution — the Admissions Committee identified you as a student of exceptional merit. Your application reflected precisely the intellectual curiosity, analytical depth, and personal initiative that define the Triton spirit.

Conditions of This Offer
  1. Continued satisfactory academic performance and successful completion of all coursework currently in progress, with no significant decline from the record upon which this offer is based.
  2. Graduation from your secondary institution with all diploma and program requirements fulfilled.
  3. Submission of a final official transcript, sent directly and under seal from your institution, no later than July 1, 2026.

To accept this offer, you must submit your Statement of Intent to Register (SIR) through the Triton Connect admissions portal no later than May 1, 2026, accompanied by the required enrollment confirmation deposit of $200.00. Please be aware that this deadline is absolute; offers not confirmed by May 1, 2026 will be rescinded and the space offered to another qualified applicant.

Your financial aid award letter, housing application portal, and orientation registration information will be posted to Triton Connect in the weeks ahead. We encourage you to review all portal items and meet each deadline without delay to ensure the best possible support for your first year at UC San Diego.

UC San Diego is a place where extraordinary things happen. From the cliffs of La Jolla to the forefront of global research, you will find here a community of thinkers and leaders ready to challenge and inspire you. We believe you will be among them — and we are proud to welcome you to the Triton family.

Sincerely,
